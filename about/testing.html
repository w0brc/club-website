Interested in becoming a licensed amateur radio operator?

Volunteer Examiner (VE) Testing

Our club is stocked with an ARRL Volunteer Examiner Team with several Amateur Extra Class VEs (necessary for any of the 3 classes of licenses). Testing for ANY class is available following our club meetings. All that we ask is that you let us know ahead of time that you wish to test and leave us your contact information.

If you need any assistance in preparing for the test, please feel free to reach out to us. We are more than willing to help.

We hope we can help you participate in our great hobby!

Club Meetings

Next Meeting Date:

Every 2nd Saturday of the month at 9:00 AM
in the Cooper County Emergency Operations Center

The club hosts monthly meetings open to everyone on the 2nd Saturday of each month at 9:00 AM in the Cooper County Emergency Operations Center located in the basement of the Cooper County Law Enforcement Center building east of the courthouse in historic downtown Boonville, MO. Please use the doorway between the courthouse and the Law Enforcement Center which faces north toward High Street and use the buzzer to state you are attending the "amateur radio club meeting" when asked.

We sincerely welcome anyone interested in amateur radio or just curious about our club to attend and hope to see you at the next meeting!

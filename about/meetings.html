Monthly Club Meetings

The club hosts monthly meetings on the 2nd Saturday of each month at 9:00 AM in the Cooper County Emergency Operations Center located in the basement of the Cooper County Law Enforcement Center building east of the courthouse in historic downtown Boonville, MO. Free parking is available on High Street just north of the courthouse. Please use the buzzer and state you are attending the "amateur radio club meeting" when asked.
